Food safety first

Safe by design, not by chance.

  • FSSAI-aligned rules: listings require a mandatory preparation-time field and are auto-blocked beyond safe time limits.
  • Hard expiry: every listing carries a hard countdown — expired food is delisted automatically, never served.
  • Good-faith donor protection: Good Samaritan-style disclaimers protect restaurants donating in good faith.
  • Verified NGOs only: NGO accounts require a registration number before claiming bulk donations.
  • OTP proof-of-handoff: a full audit trail of who collected what, and when.

📜 Listing rules (enforced)

⏲️ No food older than 4 hours at listing time
🌡️ Hot food held above 60°C / cold below 5°C
📝 Preparation time is a mandatory field
🚫 Auto-delist at expiry — no exceptions
✅ FSSAI licence number required for restaurants
